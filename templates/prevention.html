Prevention & Care

Proactive steps to protect your nerves

The #1 Rule: Blood Sugar Control

Strict control of blood glucose is the only proven method to prevent or delay the onset of diabetic neuropathy. Consistent monitoring and adhering to your prescribed diabetes management plan is crucial.

Daily Foot Checks

Check feet daily for blisters, cuts, bruises, cracked skin, redness, and swelling. Use a mirror to see the bottom of your feet.

Proper Footwear

Wear clean, dry socks. Avoid tight bands. Never walk barefoot. Wear comfortable, well-fitting shoes with plenty of room.

Healthy Diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Proper nutrition helps maintain nerve health.

Quit Smoking

Smoking constricts blood vessels, depriving peripheral nerves of the nutrient-rich blood they need to survive.
