What is Diabetic Peripheral Neuropathy?

Diabetic Peripheral Neuropathy (DPN) is a type of nerve damage that can occur if you have diabetes. High blood sugar can injure nerves throughout your body, but it most often damages nerves in your legs and feet.

Early detection is critical. Our machine learning model analyzes your vital health metrics and symptoms to provide an accurate risk assessment, helping you and your healthcare provider take proactive measures.
